Aces Marine & Salvage —
Born on the Water.

Every great marine company has a story. Ours started like many in Northern Michigan — with a truck, a trailer, and a reputation for showing up when others wouldn't.

Founded in 2010, Aces Marine & Salvage began by setting moorings and recovering boats, snowmobiles, and personal items from the bottom of the lakes. We earned our stripes one dive at a time. Today, we're a full-service marine company — but our roots are still on the bottom.

We're proud to be an authorized retailer for ShoreStation, FLOE, and Twin Bay — three of the most trusted names in dock and boat lift equipment. Whether you need a custom dock system designed, a seasonal lift installation, or emergency salvage, we bring the same work ethic to every job.

Our owner, Kyle Thompson, is a certified SCUBA instructor who has been teaching and diving since 2014. When you call Aces, you're getting someone who knows the water — literally from the bottom up.
